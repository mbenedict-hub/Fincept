Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-29.

Fincept is committed to presenting financial research content in a clean, modern, and user-friendly interface while respecting user privacy. This policy describes how website information may be collected, stored, and used.

1. Information we may collect

  • Basic contact details submitted through support or lead forms.
  • Usage analytics such as page views, browser types, and on-site interactions.
  • Preference data such as saved dashboards, watchlists, or FAQ settings where supported.

2. How we use information

  • To improve product experience, page performance, and customer support.
  • To personalize dashboard views, saved preferences, and customer workflows.
  • To communicate feature updates, policy changes, or account-related notices where permitted.

3. Cookies and analytics

Cookies, browser storage, or analytics tools may be used to remember settings, keep users signed in, and understand product engagement. In this demo version, some settings are saved locally in browser storage for preview purposes.

4. Data sharing

Fincept does not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers that help operate hosting, analytics, customer communications, or security monitoring, subject to contractual safeguards.

5. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures should be used to protect customer information. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Your choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable law allows.
  • You may manage cookie settings through your browser and device preferences.
  • You may opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe instructions in those messages.
