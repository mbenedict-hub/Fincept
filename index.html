The competitive advantage to win new users

Conviction Journal™ is the standout feature. Users can save the thesis behind any investment, define the catalysts they expect, assign confidence, set invalidation criteria, and compare the original reasoning with what actually happened. It solves a problem that many research sites under-serve: helping customers become better decision-makers rather than just faster data consumers.

Why it matters: explainable AI in finance is most persuasive when users can see assumptions, evidence, and reasoning trails instead of trusting a black box. [Source](https://rpc.cfainstitute.org/research/reports/2025/explainable-ai-in-finance)
